About Us

Core competencies, company information, and the agencies we serve.

New Century Technologies Inc. (NCT) is a Woman-Owned, Small Disadvantaged Business, EDWOSB, and GSA-certified MAS Contractor selling office products, industrial products, office furniture, appliances, toner cartridges, and IT products that offer hardware, software, and related services to government agencies. NCT is a well-established and highly regarded firm dedicated to serving government agencies at the federal, state, and local levels. With years of experience in the industry, NCT has developed a deep understanding of the specific challenges and requirements that government entities face.

Core competencies

Products

A catalog tailored to government needs: office supplies, technology hardware, software solutions, industrial equipment, appliances, safety gear, and more, sourced from trusted manufacturers, ensuring quality and reliability.

Government compliance

An understanding of government regulations, procurement procedures, and compliance requirements, allowing NCT to ensure that products and services provided align with government standards.

Sourcing and procurement

Cost-effective and efficient solutions with a proven track record of delivering on time and within budget, working closely with clients to identify their specific requirements.

Government certifications

Relevant certifications and contracts, including GSA Schedule contracts and other small business designations, enabling agencies to leverage these contracts for streamlined procurement.

Commitment to quality

Quality assurance is at the forefront of NCT. We maintain strict quality control standards, ensuring that all products and services meet or exceed government requirements and industry standards.
